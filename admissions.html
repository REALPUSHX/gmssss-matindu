Admission Process

Simple Steps to Join Our School

Follow these simple steps to complete the admission process for your child.

01

Application Form

Collect the admission form from the school office or download it when available.

02

Submit Documents

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents before the deadline.

03

Verification

The school verifies the submitted documents and confirms eligibility for admission.

04

Admission Confirmed

After successful verification, complete the admission formalities and begin your journey.

Admission Details

Eligibility & Required Documents

Please ensure that all eligibility requirements are fulfilled and the necessary documents are submitted during the admission process.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Admission is based on the school's admission policy.
  • Students must meet the age criteria for the respective class.
  • Previous academic records may be required.
  • Availability of seats is subject to school regulations.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Aadhaar Card (Student)
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Parent/Guardian Identity Proof
  • Residence Proof

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for admission?

Students who meet the age and eligibility criteria specified by the school can apply for admission.

Are original documents required?

Yes. Original documents are required only for verification during the admission process.

How will I know if admission is confirmed?

The school administration will notify parents after successful verification of documents and completion of the admission process.

Need Help With Admission?

Our admission office is happy to answer your questions and guide you through every step of the admission process.
